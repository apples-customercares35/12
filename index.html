ABOUT QUALITY CAR WASH

The Essenburg Family established Quality Car Wash in 1969 in Holland, MI. Quality Car Wash offers a variety of services, including exterior car washes, Express Interior Cleaning, fuel, Tim Hortons, and high quality products in our convenience stores. We are committed to providing exceptional guest experiences, and pride ourselves on the relationships we have built within our communities with individuals, businesses, and organizations. Still owned by the Essenburg Family, Quality Car Wash has grown to nine locations in the West Michigan area, and we are continually expanding within the market with new locations.
